Our Story

Providence, January 2012

Jared & Michela's story begins in Providence, RI in January 2012. They met online and quickly discovered that they were neighbors! When Jared picked up Michela for their first date, he only needed to walk a few houses down. Michela moved back to Boston a year later and it wasn't until the summer of 2015 that they reconnected after a serendipitous Facebook message was sent. They now share a home together in New Bedford with their two cats, Buffy and Giles. Jared and Michela are so excited to spend the rest of their lives together and can't wait to share their wedding day with all of you!

Accommodations

We can't wait for you to arrive!

We've arranged a block of rooms at the Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott, under Booker-Gardner Wedding. This waterfront hotel is a very short walk from our venue as well as lots of other attractions in downtown New Bedford!

The hotel offers free, buffet style hot breakfast to all guests. If you would like, please join Jared and Michela for breakfast from 8:30-10:00 AM on Monday morning.

For guests staying at the Fairfield Inn & Suites, Greasy Luck is a 12 minute walk from the hotel. For those who plan to drive, there is ample parking near Greasy Luck. Parking options include the Elm Street Parking Garage located at 51 Elm Street in New Bedford, the free parking lot of Citizens Bank at 95 William Street in New Bedford, and free street parking on Purchase St and surrounding streets.
